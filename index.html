Featured Project

XPEDITIONVR: Cerro Castillo

Lucid Dreams · Nov 2024

C# Unity XR Meta SDK

About the project

Full body tracking & free roam VR experience aimed to work in a real, physical location, aligning real world with a virtual environment to generate maximum immersion.

My Role

Using Unity, I implemented full-body tracking systems based on hand tracking and Pro Touch controllers, which were later deprecated after integrating Meta's Movement SDK. I also worked with spatial anchors for world alignment and initial setup, as well as 3D scanning of climbing holds.

Skills Applied

  • Integration of Meta Movement SDK
  • Spatial anchors for world alignment
  • 3D scanning
  • VR standalone application optimization
  • Spline-based animation systems
  • Hand tracking rigging and setup
  • Audio implementation and sound design
